Whom do you call after an emergency transformer failure?


Transformer failure is not common but in an unlikely situation when it does happen, look no further than the experts at ABC. We’ll quickly determine the most effective solution for your infrastructure’s unique needs. Repair Services

Reliable

Cost Effective

Skillfully Re-Engineered


Our Transformer repair process encompasses the following stages:


  • Detanking
  • JVR – Joint Verification report is made. (Joint and open inspection of the extent of damage.)
  • New Windings
  • Cleaning and Re assembly of Core
  • Complete Insulation Change
  • Full tank overhaul and replacement of all seals and gaskets
  • New Bushings, Metal parts and other accessories where required
  • Tap switch assessment and Service
  • Oil replacement / filtration depending upon the condition
  • Ovening of Transformer Assembly
  • Re-assembly and painting
  • Complete Transformer Testing as per IS 2026

    • We are the best choice to repair / rewind, or remanufacture your Oil cooled, dry-type or any other special purpose transformer.
